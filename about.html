About Me

I'm Danielle "Elle" Stewart, an undergraduate student at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. I am a junior pursuing a B.S. in Creative Technologies-Interdisciplinary Art and a B.S. in Computer Science-Web Computing.

Currently, I hold the positions of Social Media Specialist for the School of Information Technolgy and the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for the Student Wellness Ambassador Team. I design and upload graphics promoting thier events, achievements, and goals.

I've had a passion for creating from a young age and am extremely grateful to be able to incorporate my creativity in my education at ISU and eventually in my future career. Some of my other hobbies include crochet, sewing, and playing Stardew Valley.
