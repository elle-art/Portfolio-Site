About Me

I'm Danielle "Elle" Stewart, an undergraduate student at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. I am a rising junior pursuing a B.S in Creative Technologies-Interdisciplinary Art and a B.S. in Web Computing.

Currently, I hold the position of Social Media Manager for the School of Information Technolgy in which I create an upload graphics pertaining to our school events. Next semester, I will also be the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for the Student Wellness Ambassador Team.

I've had a passion for creating from a young age and am extremely grateful to be able to incorporate my creativity in my education at ISU and eventually in my future career. Some of my other hobbies include crochet, sewing, and playing Stardew Valley.
