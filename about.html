About Me

I'm Danielle "Elle" Stewart, a Web Developer located in Normal, Illinois. This Spring I graduated from Illinois State University with a B.S. in Computer Science - Web Computing and a B.S. in Creative Technologies - Interdisciplinary Art.

Currently, I am seeking full-time opportunities in web development and front-end development. I am passionate about creating accessible, user-friendly, and visually appealing web applications that provide meaningful experiences for users.

I currently serve as a Website Administrator for Campers Inn RV and have also worked as a Student Web Developer for the College of Arts & Sciences IT department. Additionally, I gained valuable experience as the Social Media Manager for the School of Information Technology, the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for the Student Wellness Ambassador Team, and the founder and Director of Operations for Compassion on Campus at ISU.

I've had a passion for creating from a young age and am extremely grateful to be able to incorporate my creativity into the web development field. Outside of work, some of my other hobbies include crochet, sewing, volunteering as a Data Analyst for Educate. Radiate. Elevate., and playing Stardew Valley.
