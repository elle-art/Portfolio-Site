About Me

I'm Danielle "Elle" Stewart, an undergraduate student at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. I am currently a senior pursuing a B.S. in Creative Technologies - Interdisciplinary Art and a B.S. in Computer Science - Web Computing. I’ll be walking at graduation in Spring 2025, though I will complete three general education classes required for my degrees in the summer.

Currently, I work as the Social Media Manager for the School of Information Technology and as a Student Web Developer for the College of Arts & Sciences IT department. I’ve also served as the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for the Student Wellness Ambassador Team and the founder and Director of Operations for Compassion on Campus at ISU.

I've had a passion for creating from a young age and am extremely grateful to be able to incorporate my creativity into my education at ISU and eventually in my future career. Outside of school and work, some of my other hobbies include crochet, sewing, and playing Stardew Valley.

Current Projects

I'm currently working on Greetings 101, a language-learning web app designed to promote inclusivity and reduce stigmas around multilingualism. The frontend of the app uses Next.js and Typescript. The backend was developed with Django.

Some of the features that have been implemented are: An interactive Spanish module, user sign up and profile customization, and user analytics.
