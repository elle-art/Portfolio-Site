About Me

I'm Danielle "Elle" Stewart, a Web Developer located in Normal, Illinois. This Spring I graduated from Illinois State University with a B.S. in Computer Science - Web Computing and a B.S. in Creative Technologies - Interdisciplinary Art. Though I still must complete two general education classes required for my CS degree over the summer, officially obtaining my second degree in July 2025.

Currently, I work as a Student Web Developer for the College of Arts & Sciences IT department. I’ve also worked as the Social Media Manager for the School of Information Technology, the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for the Student Wellness Ambassador Team, and the founder and Director of Operations for Compassion on Campus at ISU.

I've had a passion for creating from a young age and am extremely grateful to be able to incorporate my creativity into my education at ISU and eventually in my future career. Outside of school and work, some of my other hobbies include crochet, sewing, and playing Stardew Valley.

Current Projects

I'm currently working on Greetings 101, a language-learning web app designed to promote inclusivity and reduce stigmas around multilingualism. The frontend of the app uses Next.js and Typescript. The backend was developed with Django.

Some of the features that have been implemented are: An interactive Spanish module, user authentication, analytics, profile customization, and a flashcard practice game. I plan to add much more in the future, but my next steps will be refining the Spanish 101 module and adding to the ASL 101 module.
